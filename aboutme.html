 About Me

Bonjour, I am Emilie

I am currently a Postdoctoral fellow at the University of Alabama in Tuscaloosa. Here is a little bit about me and my interests outside of astronomy!

Baker

I've been a baker for many years now, it all started with my grandma when I was a small kid. I bake any kind of pastry, bread or dessert in general. I love sharing what I make with my friends and family (or anyone really) and will often bring food to work or to the gym.

Bouldering

I started bouldering at the beginning of my PhD, during lunch breaks with other people from the lab, mostly because I wanted to make friends. I completely fell in love with that sport and before long I was the one encouraging young PhDs to join the gang. And my goal got completed: I made a lot of climbing buddies along the way! Side-note: if you're in the Tuscaloosa area you should totally check out the Crux climbing gym, immaculate vibes.

Books

I'm an avid reader. I could read absolutely any genres, so if you have any book recommendations, feel free to share! Recent favorites include "Braiding sweetgrass" by Robin Wall Kimmerer or "The blue castle" by Lucy Maud Montgomery.
