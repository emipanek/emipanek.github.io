Conclusions

  • No single heuristic is always optimal
    • time greedy is best if the goal is to maximize the number of planets
    • binning or clustering methods can give structured coverage of parameter space
    • leverage and variance greedy are better for diversity but can overly emphasize rare cases
    • Simulated annealing could provide a compromise?
  • Is the leverage really aligning with Ariel scientific goals?
    • Combining leverage with other metrics?
