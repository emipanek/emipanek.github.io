Emilie PANEK

Astrophysicist
Postdoctoral researcher

Hello! I'm an astrophysicist focusing on exoplanets, meaning planets outside our good old Solar system. I'm working with space data from telescopes like the Hubble Space Telescope or the James Webb Space Telescope. I'm also part of the consortium for the future Ariel mission, which will be launched in 2029. I love making science more accessible to everyone by doing outreach projects like conferences, quizzes or meetings with the general public. Originally French but now living in Tuscaloosa, Alabama, I love baking! You can also find me wandering around in the wild or at the climbing gym, joyfully crimping on plastic holds.
